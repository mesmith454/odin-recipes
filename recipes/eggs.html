Eggs


Description:

Scrambled like my brain


Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Crack eggs and froth with fork.
  2. Dice them onions and tomatoes
  3. Heat the pan and butter that bitch
  4. Mix your salt'n'pepper and cheese with the egg froth
  5. Slightly brown onions in pan
  6. Toss in egg mix and scramble until fluffy and not liquidy
